Arquivo Urbano

This project consisted of creation of a visual identity for a Museum named Arquivo Urbano, the visual identity is based off of a detail in a painting, where the element was turned into the logo, and several patterns, the color pallete also takes inspiration from the painting. Several Digital, Print and Physical objects were created to showcase the visual identity in different mediums.
